Lemon tea is a variant of tea made with lime juice, then served with a lemon wedge. This drink has a sweet taste mixed with lemon acid. Ice lemon tea is very suitable to be enjoyed when the weather is hot.

Ingredient :

  • 1 small pack of brewed tea
  • 1/2 lemon, take the water
  • 3 tablespoons of sugar / according to taste
  • 500 ml of water
  • enough ice cubes

Steps :

  1. Cook brewed tea with boiling water, until it is reddish in color and turn off the heat.
  2. Put sugar and lemon juice into a glass.
  3. Put the lemon pieces in a glass, and pour the tea while straining, stir until the sugar dissolves.
  4. Add ice cubes to taste, serve.
