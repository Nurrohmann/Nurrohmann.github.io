Sweet iced tea is almost on every restaurant drink menu in Indonesia. However, restaurant-style sweet iced tea is usually different from what you make at home.

In addition, the sugar is also boiled beforehand so it dissolves easily in the iced tea and the sweetness feels just right. Check out the restaurant-style sweet iced tea recipe from Sajian Sedap below.

Ingredient :

  • 400 ml of water
  • 50 grams of brewed tea (not tea bags), preferably a mix of different brands
  • 500 grams of ice cubes
  • 3 pandan leaves tied a knot
  • 300 grams of sugar
  • 300 ml of vanilla paste water (optional)

Steps

  1. Bring 400 milliliters of water to a boil, then pour in the tea. Cook five minutes over low heat, turn off the stove. Set aside and do not strain the tea, let it further dissolve.
  2. Continue to make the syrup ingredients, bring to a boil 300 milliliters of water. Add sugar and pandan leaves. Cook until the sugar dissolves, help by stirring.
  3. Prepare a glass, fill ice cubes in a ratio of one to one with tea water, for example 100 grams of ice cubes to one hundred milliliters of tea water. Strain the tea, pour it into a glass and add sugar syrup to taste.
